Nov 2025 · Bangkok
Index & handoff note

Six artifacts, one receipt.

Pawpoint is a booking spine for Bangkok's pet-care market — built around Shield, a refundable deposit that makes every confirmation real. This package is the whole argument: one customer app, one merchant console, one ops room, a brand layer, a stakeholder one-pager, and a public landing page. Everything here is a working prototype package — open the surfaces, inspect the tweaks, and look at the numbers. Start wherever is useful; the pages cross-reference each other where the details rhyme.

How it fits together

One spine, six surfaces.

Every artifact shares the same tokens, the same Shield mechanic, the same Bangkok-specific copy. The design system is the source of truth: the other five consume it. When you see copper, it's the same copper. When you see a Shield badge, it behaves the same way.

The three product surfaces (Customer, Merchant, Ops) are three views of one booking. A booking created on the customer app lights up as a pending request on the merchant iPad, and shows up on the ops pulse if something goes wrong.

The one-pager is the pitch and the landing page is the public face — both tell the same story at different altitudes.

  • Shared
    Tokens, type, Shield mechanic, copy style — all five product artifacts link to shared/pawpoint-tokens.css
  • Cross-ref
    Each surface calls out the others by name — booking IDs, shop names, and pet names repeat across the three product artifacts
  • Localisation
    Bilingual Thai/English throughout — toggle via Tweaks on the customer app; Thai is primary in shop-facing surfaces
  • Fidelity
    Hi-fi guided walkthroughs for the three product surfaces — one-pager and landing page are pixel-final print/web
Where it goes next

Three things to sharpen before the Feb ship.

01 · Shield v2
Delayed capture, not pre-auth.

Move from day-of pre-authorisation to 72-hour delayed capture. Halves chargebacks; removes the “why is there a charge on my card” support thread.

02 · Cat vertical
Separate discovery flow.

Six feline-specialist shops are already onboard. They need their own filter, their own landing surface, and cat-specific service taxonomies. Separate the flow before it contaminates the default.

03 · Vet wedge
Same Shield, new category.

Bangkok's veterinary bookings look exactly like grooming with one extra field. Pilot in Feb with three clinics. If Shield holds up for shots and check-ups, the market doubles overnight.
