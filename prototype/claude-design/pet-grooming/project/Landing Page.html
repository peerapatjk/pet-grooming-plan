Booking a groomer in Bangkok shouldn't be a group chat.

Today, scheduling a bath or full groom means ping-ponging across LINE, Instagram, Facebook, and the shop's phone. Nobody can tell if a slot is actually yours until someone replies. Pawpoint fixes the coordination, not the craft.

03 · Trust, explained

Payment protection in plain language.

We use card holds and — for higher-risk cases — small deposits to cut no-shows. These are protections, not penalties. Pawpoint is up-front about every rule, every timer, and every way to get your hold released.

¶
Card hold, not a charge

Routine bookings place a temporary card authorisation. Nothing is captured until the appointment completes — and the hold lifts automatically if you cancel in the free window.

Authorisation · no capture
§
Deposits, only when they fit

Full grooms or higher-value services may ask for a small deposit. The amount, the refund window, and what happens on a no-show are stated before you commit.

Explicit policy · per service
✦
Reminders & reconfirm

You get a friendly reminder the day before and the morning of. A single tap reconfirms. If you can't make it, cancelling is the same one tap — no phone call required.

04 · For grooming shops

One canonical schedule. Not six chat windows.

Pawpoint's merchant app replaces the mental tracking and LINE archaeology with a single board that every booking — online, phone, walk-in — lives inside.

01
Truthful availability

You set routine services, durations, online-booking hours, and cut-offs. The slots customers see are the slots you actually have.

02
Offline bookings live in the same board

Phone call, LINE message, walk-in — capture the booking inside Pawpoint and it shares the same slot logic and payment protection.

03
Request-confirm, not first-come-first-hope

High-variance jobs route for your review. Approve, decline with an explicit reason, or let the timeout expire cleanly — the outcome is always auditable.

04
No-shows get quieter

Card holds and deposits do the work reminders can't. You get paid for the slot even when the customer doesn't show — and the policy is never a surprise.

05 · What's launching

A deliberately narrow start.

We're launching a tight slice on purpose. Pawpoint is Bangkok-first, in-shop only, and focused on a small set of premium-leaning independent shops in dense neighborhoods. Breadth can wait until the core booking is actually trusted.

WHERE · AREA

Central Bangkok,
dense neighborhoods.

Sathorn, Thonglor, Ari, Phrom Phong, and Sukhumvit sois — clusters where a great shop is minutes away, not an expedition.

Not yet: suburbs, other cities
WHO · SHOPS

Independent shops,
premium-leaning.

Owner-operated grooming studios who care about craft, already have repeat clientele, and are tired of running a schedule out of LINE.

Not yet: chain salons, franchise PET+
WHAT · SERVICES

In-shop grooming,
one pet at a time.

Bath, tidy, full grooms, nails, ear care — for one pet with an optional set of fixed add-ons. Predictable enough to actually be truthful about slots.

Not yet: home service, multi-pet, bundles
