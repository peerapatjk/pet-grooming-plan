Pawpoint · Stakeholder briefing
Apr 2026 · Confidential

Bangkok's grooming book is a group chat. We're building the receipt.

In Bangkok, booking a groomer is a LINE message, a shrug, and a drive across town hoping the shop remembers. Fifty-eight percent of pet parents we interviewed said they've shown up to a salon that had no record of the booking. Shops, meanwhile, lose an hour a day to phone tag and a third of their Saturdays to no-shows.

Pawpoint is a booking spine for the whole market — one customer app, one shop console, one ops room — held together by Shield, a refundable deposit that makes every confirmation real. We are 41 shops and 1,842 pet parents into Bangkok. Saturday retention is up 22 points. The next twelve weeks are the ones that matter.
