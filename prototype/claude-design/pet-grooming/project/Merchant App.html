Pawpoint Merchant · iPad counter app · operations

The shop runs calm, because the hard moments are boring.

Pawpoint’s merchant surface is tuned for one place: the counter of a busy Bangkok grooming shop. The owner sees the day. Staff see the board. Incoming booking requests — the “pending” moment that makes Pawpoint work — resolve in under a minute, with every piece of context on one sheet. Everything uses shared tokens from the design system so color, status, and type stay canonical with the customer app.
