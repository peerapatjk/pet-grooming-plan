Ch. 03 · the mechanic · Shield
Shield

A deposit that evaporates
the moment the dog arrives.

A 200-baht hold is placed on the card at booking (shops set their own hold; ฿200 is the Aroon Grooming default). Not a charge. When the customer scans the shop's QR on arrival, the hold releases automatically — the shop sees confirmation, the customer sees nothing. No awkward conversation about money; no no-shows.
T – 3 days · booking
฿200
Refundable hold placed. Customer's card is held, not charged. A bank-level auth, not a transaction.
→
On arrival · QR scan
QR
Customer scans the shop's QR at the counter. Hold releases to shop in full. Zero tap for both sides.
→
If no-show · T + 24h
฿450
Shop is paid for the lost slot. Customer gets one strike before they're locked out of the platform.
Net effect: merchants stop accepting bookings they don't trust; customers stop ghosting shops they've never paid. The platform handles the awkwardness — so humans don't have to.
