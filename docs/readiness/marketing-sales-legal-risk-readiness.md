# Marketing Sales Legal And Risk Readiness

## Purpose

This packet defines what commercial, policy, and control functions need before the launch slice can be presented or sold honestly.

Use it to align claims, trust copy, merchant promises, terms, and review expectations.

## Status

- Maturity: `draft`
- Current state: template only; no named owners, evidence, or approval dates yet
- Owner: TBD

## Packet Sections

### Marketing packet

- launch audience and wedge statement
- approved claims and prohibited claims
- trust messaging for holds, deposits, pending states, cancellations, and no-shows

### Sales / BD packet

- merchant promise sheet
- onboarding expectations and support model
- target-list assumptions for the initial wedge

### Legal packet

- customer terms
- merchant terms
- consent copy for OTP, notifications, and payment protection
- dispute and policy-language review list

### Risk / Privacy / Security packet

- risky-flow review checklist
- access-control and audit expectations
- retention and privacy posture summary
- required control signoffs

## Required Decisions

- Which launch claims are approved, and which are out of bounds?
- How is payment protection explained without sounding punitive or misleading?
- Which merchant promises can Sales / BD make during the launch wedge?
- Which customer and merchant obligations must appear in launch-ready terms?
- Which risky flows require explicit review or waiver before launch?

## Evidence Required Before Launch

- reviewed landing-page, customer-flow, and one-pager language aligned to the canon
- approved trust-copy set for holds, deposits, pending states, and merchant disruptions
- launch-like terms and consent review
- documented control expectations for override access, privacy, and auditability

## Exit Criteria

- Marketing approves launch claims and trust language
- Sales / BD approves merchant promises and target assumptions
- Legal approves terms, policy wording, and consent language
- Risk / Privacy / Security approves required controls or issues explicit waivers
- named owners, dates, and unresolved issues are copied into [../../spec-driven-development/stakeholder-readiness-status.md](../../spec-driven-development/stakeholder-readiness-status.md)
